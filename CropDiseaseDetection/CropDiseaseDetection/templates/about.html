🌱 About Crop.Health

🔍 What is Crop.Health?

Crop.Health is an AI-powered platform designed to assist farmers and agricultural experts in detecting and managing crop diseases efficiently. By leveraging advanced machine learning, our system provides instant disease analysis and treatment suggestions, ensuring higher productivity and sustainability.

🛠️ How It Works

  • AI-Based Disease Detection: Upload an image of your crop, and our AI will analyze it for potential diseases.
  • Expert Insights: Read expert-written blogs and stay updated on the latest agricultural trends.
  • Community Support: Connect with fellow farmers, researchers, and agricultural experts to share knowledge.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for easy navigation, even for first-time users.
